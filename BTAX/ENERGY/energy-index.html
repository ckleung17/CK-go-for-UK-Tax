Oil, Gas, Electricity and Environmental Taxation

This study area covers the permanent upstream oil and gas tax regime, temporary and proposed sector levies, electricity-generation taxes and selected environmental taxes. Each module will distinguish company-level, field-level and transaction-level charges and state the relevant territory, accounting period and effective date.

Status at 20 July 2026: the Energy Profits Levy is scheduled to end on 31 March 2030, or earlier if the Energy Security Investment Mechanism is triggered. HMRC published draft Oil and Gas Revenue Levy legislation on 13 July 2026 for a successor charge. The announced Electricity Generator Levy increase from 45% to 55% applies to receipts attributable to generation from 1 July 2026, subject to the implementing legislation and transitional rules. These status points must be rechecked before relying on a later version of this page.
