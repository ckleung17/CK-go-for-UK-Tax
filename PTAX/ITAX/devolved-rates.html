Scotland

Scottish non-savings, non-dividend taxable slices use 19% to £3,967; 20% to £16,956; 21% to £31,092; 42% to £62,430; 45% to £125,140; and 48% above. Savings and dividends use UK-wide rates.

Wales

Welsh rates remain 10p in each band, producing combined 20%, 40% and 45% rates for 2026/27.
