Making Tax Digital and Digital Tax Compliance

This study area covers the legal and operational requirements for digital records, compatible software, digital submissions and related compliance across VAT and Income Tax, together with developing Corporation Tax and e-invoicing proposals.

Status at 20 July 2026: Making Tax Digital (MTD) for Income Tax became mandatory from 6 April 2026 for qualifying individuals whose 2024/25 qualifying income exceeded £50,000. The scheduled thresholds are more than £30,000 for entry from 6 April 2027 and more than £20,000 for entry from 6 April 2028. MTD for Corporation Tax remains consultation-only. Separate proposals to modernise and standardise Company Tax Returns were the subject of a consultation that closed on 2 June 2026, while the former joint online accounts and Company Tax Return service closed on 31 March 2026.
