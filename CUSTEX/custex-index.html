Scope of this study area

This index covers the UK customs framework, Import VAT, excise duties and associated compliance and dispute procedures. The applicable rules can differ between Great Britain, Northern Ireland and the Isle of Man and may depend on the origin, destination and movement date of the goods.

The headings below are the roadmap for detailed study modules. Northern Ireland arrangements, tariff measures and operational customs procedures can change independently of the underlying legislation, so each module will state its territorial scope, effective date and source status.
