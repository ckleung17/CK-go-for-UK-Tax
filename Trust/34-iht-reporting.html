Operational framework

Study checkpoint: requirements current on 17 July 2026. The IHT100 suite reports specified lifetime and trust chargeable events. Separate event forms cover entry transfers, termination of qualifying interests, exits, ten-year anniversaries and other occasions. Trustees must test both liability and delivery exceptions rather than filing every transaction automatically.

For each Inheritance Tax event, identify the correct event form, test any delivery threshold, support the valuation, and diary the statutory deadline.

The detailed analysis begins with Event form, which provides the foundation for the concepts that follow.

Reporting an Inheritance Tax trust event

This topic turns on four linked questions. Resolve them in sequence using the governing instrument, event chronology and supporting evidence, because the answer to one question can alter the next.

Event form

IHT100a–IHT100g identify the event category; the main account supplies parties, values, history and calculation information.

That conclusion leads to the next question: delivery threshold.

Delivery threshold

Some events need not be delivered where statutory or administrative conditions are met, but records must support the conclusion.

That conclusion leads to the next question: valuation.

Valuation

Use the event-date open-market value, liabilities and relief evidence, including related-property and shareholding effects.

That conclusion leads to the next question: deadline.

Deadline

For relevant post-6 April 2014 trust events, reporting and payment are generally due by the end of the sixth month after the event.

Taken together, these concepts determine which records, checks and safeguards are needed in practice.

Practical issues and safeguards

A nil tax result does not always mean no account, and a taxable event may require TRS updates as well as IHT100. Use the current event form and notes rather than a historic generic IHT100 pack.

  • Identify the exact chargeable event and current event-specific form.
  • Calculate tax and delivery requirements separately because nil tax may still require reporting.
  • Diarise valuation, filing, payment, TRS update and amendment responsibilities.
