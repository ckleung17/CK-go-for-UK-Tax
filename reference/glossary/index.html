How abbreviations are used

On first use in a study topic, write the full term followed immediately by its abbreviation in brackets. The abbreviation may then be used within that topic where its meaning remains clear.

Example: National Insurance contributions (NICs), followed by NICs later in the same topic.

Technical words without abbreviations, such as slice, are also included so they can be used consistently and with their intended tax meaning.
